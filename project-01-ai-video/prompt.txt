Goal:
Generate high-quality 8-second AI video clips for a business automation explainer,
intended for professional and corporate use.

Video Style & Aesthetics:
- Corporate, clean, and minimal visual style
- Neutral and professional color palette (soft grays, whites, muted blues)
- Soft, natural lighting with no harsh shadows
- Realistic environments and human proportions
- Cinematic but subtle look, avoiding stylized or cartoonish visuals

Camera & Composition:
- Stable camera with smooth, slow movements only
- Professional framing (medium and wide shots)
- Balanced composition following rule of thirds
- No fast cuts or abrupt camera movements

Motion & Timing:
- Natural, slow-paced motion suitable for an 8-second clip
- Clear visual focus within the first 2 seconds
- Smooth transitions within the scene

Mood & Emotion:
- Professional, realistic, and business-oriented
- Calm and credible emotional tone
- Avoid exaggerated facial expressions or gestures

Constraints:
- Avoid futuristic or sci-fi elements
- Avoid exaggerated animations or effects
- Avoid visual distortions, warped faces, or unrealistic movements
- Avoid overly bright or saturated colors
